EverDesktop

A small Mac application for clipping files on your Desktop to Evernote.

Available on the Mac App Store

Keep your Mac tidy.

EverDesktop allows you to make tidy your Mac from status bar icon.

Clip to Evernote.

Clipped files will be attached and embed in notes.

Just moves files.

After clipping files, EverDesktop moves them to new individual folders based on date and time.
